Even in the vastness of space, wise travellers prepare for space weather's unpredictable embrace

Interstellar Journeys is a dedicated team of explorers, scientists, and visionaries united by the dream of interstellar travel. Our mission is to inspire and inform, advancing the science and technology needed to make star travel a reality. Along this extraordinary journey, we remain vigilant about the hazards of space, including cosmic radiation and the vast distances involved, and we work to raise awareness among astronomers and space enthusiasts about these challenges. Join us as we explore the cosmos, search for new worlds, and envision a future where humanity reaches for the stars, turning science fiction into fact.
